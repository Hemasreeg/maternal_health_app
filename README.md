ðŸ’¡ **Key Features**

**1.Pregnancy Week Calculator**
       Users enter the first day of their last menstrual period (LMP).
       
      The app calculates:
      
           Current week of pregnancy
           
           Estimated due date
           
           Days remaining until delivery
           

           
**2.Stage-Based Health Tips**
      Weekly guidance tailored to trimesters:
      
     First Trimester: Folic acid, diet, early checkups
     
     Second Trimester: Iron intake, movement tracking
    
    Third Trimester: Hospital bag, danger signs

    
**3.Danger Signs Alert**

    Clear warnings for symptoms requiring immediate medical attention:
    
        Severe headache, blurred vision
         
        Vaginal bleeding
         
        Swelling in hands/face
         
        Reduced fetal movement
         
**4.Simple & Private**

      No data collection or storage
      
      Fully anonymous
      
      Works on mobile phones and computers
      
      Lightweight & Deployable
      
      No database or backend required
      
      Can be deployed free online using Streamlit Community Cloud
      
      Easily shared via link (e.g., WhatsApp, clinics, schools)
